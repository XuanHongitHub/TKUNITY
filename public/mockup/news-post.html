Season 4 Championship Begins

The biggest tournament of the year is here with a prize pool of $100,000. Register now and compete for glory across all supported games.

TK

TKUnity Team

Official Announcement

The moment you've all been waiting for is finally here. TKUnity is proud to announce the start of Season 4 Championship, our biggest competitive gaming event of the year. With a massive prize pool of $100,000, this season promises to be the most exciting yet.

"This season represents our commitment to the competitive gaming community. We've listened to your feedback and made significant improvements to the tournament structure, prizing, and overall experience."

What's New in Season 4

We've made several major changes based on community feedback from Season 3. Here's what you can expect:

Key Updates

• Increased prize pool from $75,000 to $100,000

• New double-elimination bracket format

• Improved anti-cheat measures

• Live streams with professional commentary

• Regional qualifiers for better ping

Prize Distribution

$100,000 Total Prize Pool

The prize pool will be distributed among the top performers in each game category:

  • 1st Place: $25,000 per game + Championship Trophy
  • 2nd Place: $12,000 per game
  • 3rd Place: $6,000 per game
  • 4th-8th Place: $2,000 per team

How to Register

Getting started is easy. Follow these steps to secure your spot in the championship:

  1. Create or Log in to your TKUnity account
  2. Navigate to the Tournaments section
  3. Select your preferred game and region
  4. Complete team registration (solo players welcome too!)
  5. Join our Discord for important updates and match schedules

Eligibility Requirements

Who Can Participate

• Players must be at least 16 years old

• Valid TKUnity account in good standing

• Minimum account level 15 (for fair matching)

• Teams must have 3-5 players depending on game

Live Coverage

All matches will be streamed live on our official channels with professional casters, analysis, and viewer-exclusive drops. Make sure to follow us on Twitch and YouTube so you don't miss any action.

Viewers can also participate in predictions and win exclusive in-game items and TKUnity credits during the broadcasts.

Sponsor Spotlight

We're grateful to our partners who make this championship possible:

This season is sponsored by leading gaming brands including Razer, ASUS ROG, and Discord. Their support helps us bring you the best competitive gaming experience possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I participate in multiple games?

Yes! You can register for as many games as you want, but make sure your team can commit to the schedule for each tournament.

What happens if I disconnect during a match?

We have pause protocols in place for technical issues. Contact a tournament admin immediately via Discord if problems occur.

Is there a registration fee?

No, participation in the Season 4 Championship is completely free for all TKUnity users.

Ready to Compete?

The Season 4 Championship is your chance to prove yourself on the biggest stage. Whether you're a seasoned pro or an aspiring competitor, this is your moment to shine.

Registration closes on January 15, 2025. Don't miss your chance to become the next TKUnity Champion!
